An empire falls! Months after the Battle of Endor, the insidious DIRECTOR ISARD has seized the vacant Imperial throne in a bloody coup.

However, many powerful and ambitious Imperials also seek the throne, and have declared war against Isard's regime, becoming renegade IMPERIAL WARLORDS.

In this chaos, the Rebellion, now reformed as the NEW REPUBLIC, aspire to unite the galaxy. Hoping to gain new allies, they have sent out forces to liberate any rim world suffering from Imperial oppression. . . .
